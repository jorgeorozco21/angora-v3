Our Story

Angora Sport Shop was founded with the vision of breaking barriers in the sportswear industry. From the beginning, we believed that everyone deserves to feel confident and empowered while pursuing their fitness journey. Our logo represents a person without a defined gender—a universal symbol of inclusivity and equality. This reflects our commitment to dress every individual, regardless of gender, who is passionate about sports and fitness.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, comfortable, and stylish sportswear for everyone. We aim to inspire people to live healthier lives and embrace the joy of movement, no matter who they are or where they come from.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in inclusive sportswear, fostering a community where everyone feels supported and motivated to achieve their fitness goals. We envision a world where sports and fitness are accessible to all, empowering individuals to push their limits and redefine what's possible.

Our Objectives

  • To innovate and offer versatile sportswear that adapts to all body types and preferences.
  • To build a community that celebrates diversity and inclusivity in sports and fitness.
  • To continually improve the sustainability of our products and processes.
  • To partner with organizations that promote active lifestyles and inclusivity worldwide.
